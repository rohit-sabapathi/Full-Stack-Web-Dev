The Best movies according to Rohit

Top 5 movies of All-Time!

The Pursuit of Happiness

The Movies enlighten us by showing the hardships of the protagonist where he becomes successful.
It depicts the struggles he went through to attain the life he wanted to live with his family.

I am Legend

This is a sci-fi movie where everybody dies, only few survives it.
The protagonist will find the antidote by the end of the movie.

I, Robot

This sci-fi movie where the future of the earth have been advanced to the development of Robots.
The protagonist will find his way to accept them as part of the society and human needs.

Hachi: A dog's tale

This movie is a true story, based on the emotional concept of feelings between a dog and a man.

Bridge to Terabithia

This is fantasy movie, where the protagonist meets the Deuteragonist, where he becomes friends with her.
This movies is also emotional where the Deuteragonist dies.
